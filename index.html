A Wolf's Journey

When speaking in the terms of my Senior Exhibition the idea I would like to bring forth to it is that of a coded timeline. While as it may seem to be a website the bigger picture behind it is that of my own personal development in the field of Graphic Design. For example, starting the Graphic Design program up until now I have seen a sense of improvement among my work. An that is what I would like to showcase in my exhibition from the development of starting my craft in Graphic Design to the present day now.
